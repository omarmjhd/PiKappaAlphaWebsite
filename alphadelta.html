Scholars

Pike strives to develop men through finding their intellectual and personal potential. Alpha Delta Pikes major in everything from Engineering and Science to Management and Architecture. Each semester brothers are named to the Dean’s List, and many brothers participate in the Georgia Tech Study Abroad programs.

Leaders

The brothers of Pi Kappa Alpha serve as leaders inside and outside the classroom. Some work at the CRC as intramural supervisors and officials while others have even opened their own companies. Pi Kappa Alpha has recognizable leaders in corporate America, such as S. Truett Cathy, Gil Amelio, and other government and high ranking military officials. General Philip M. Breedlove ’79 recently joined us for our 1st Annual Barbecue for Breast Cancer Awareness.

Athletes

Each year Pike is the team to beat. We compete in every intramural sport, and Pike recently won school champion- ships in both volleyball and wallyball. Also we finished runner-up for the intramural cup for the 2010-2011 school year.

Gentlemen

Pikes are well-known as gentlemen on campus and in the IFC. Each semester Pike has mixers with sororities on campus, and in the fall we had brothers participate in TeamBuzz and other philanthropy events. This past fall, Pike held its 1st Annual Barbecue for Breast Cancer Awareness, which was a great success.
