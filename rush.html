Rush Pike

Scholars, Leaders, Athletes, and Gentlemen

More

A fraternity, like any organization, can only be as great as the members of which it is comprised. Pi Kappa Alpha is the largest international fraternity with more than 220 chapters and colonies across the United States and Canada. Our goals in rush are to always strive to recruit “the most best men,” in quantity and quality.

At Pike, we aim to excel academically while also excelling in our personal lives. We recruit high quality men that exhibit characteristics from the acronym SLAG: scholars, leaders, athletes and gentlemen.

If you uphold high standards in these core qualities, then contact us at rush@gtpikes.com to find out more about our rush events.
