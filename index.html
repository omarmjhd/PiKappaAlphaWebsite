Pi Kappa Alpha

Scholars, Leaders, Athletes, Gentlemen

Pi Kappa Alpha was founded at the University of Virginia on March 1st, 1868, making it one of the oldest and largest fraternities today. The Alpha Delta Chapter was founded at the Georgia Institute of Technology in 1904. Since then, Georgia Tech Pikes have been involved throughout campus organizations, led intramural sporting events, and excelled academically. Our alumni network stretches across the globe, providing benefits that extend beyond graduation from Georgia Tech.
